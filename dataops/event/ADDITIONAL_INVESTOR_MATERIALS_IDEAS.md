# Additional Investor Materials - Ideas

**Current Assets**: Earnings call transcripts, analyst reports, financial reports, infographics, emails, annual reports  
**Question**: What else would investors want?

---

## Tier 1: High-Value Investor Materials

### 1. Investor Presentation Decks

**What**: PowerPoint/PDF slide decks for investor meetings

**Types**:
- **Q2/Q3 Investor Deck** - Quarterly business updates
- **Investor Day Presentation** - Annual strategic overview (different from earnings call)
- **Series Funding Decks** - For private companies (ICBG, QRYQ, etc.)
- **IPO Roadshow Deck** - For companies going public

**Content for Each Company**:
- Company overview (1-2 slides)
- Market opportunity (TAM/SAM/SOM)
- Product screenshots and demo
- Revenue growth charts (already have SVGs!)
- Competitive positioning
- Customer logos and testimonials
- Financial highlights
- Team bios with photos
- Strategic roadmap
- Investment thesis (why invest)

**Format**: 15-30 slides per company

**Example**:
```
SNOW_Investor_Deck_Q2_FY2025.pdf
- Slide 1: Cover (Snowflake logo, date)
- Slide 2: Safe harbor statement
- Slide 3: Company overview & vision
- Slide 4: Market opportunity ($165B TAM)
- Slide 5: Product platform architecture
- Slide 6: Revenue growth chart (our SVG!)
- Slide 7: Key metrics dashboard
- Slide 8: Customer growth
- Slide 9: Customer logos (Fortune 500)
- Slide 10: Competitive advantages vs ICBG, QRYQ
- Slide 11: Cortex AI differentiation
- Slide 12: Partner ecosystem
- Slide 13: Financial highlights
- Slide 14: Guidance for FY2026
- Slide 15: Investment thesis
```

### 2. Quarterly Earnings Press Releases

**What**: Official announcements (separate from earnings call transcripts)

**Types**:
- **Q2 FY2025 Earnings Release** - August 2024
- **Q3 FY2025 Earnings Release** - November 2024
- **NRNT Bankruptcy Announcement** - November 20, 2024

**Format**: 2-3 page press releases

**Content**:
- Headline with key numbers
- CEO quote
- CFO quote
- Financial tables
- Business highlights
- Guidance
- Conference call details
- Contact information

**Example**:
```
SNOW_Q2_FY2025_Earnings_Release.pdf
- Revenue $900M, up 29% YoY
- Product revenue $856M, up 31%
- RPO $3.8B, up 44%
- CEO quote about Cortex AI launch
- CFO quote about path to profitability
- Full financial tables
- "NRNT threat dismissed as irrelevant to enterprise"
```

### 3. Investor Fact Sheets (One-Pagers)

**What**: Single-page summary of key metrics

**Content**:
- Company logo and tagline
- Elevator pitch (2 sentences)
- Stock ticker and price
- Key metrics (4-6 numbers)
- Revenue chart
- Customer count
- Quick facts
- Competitive advantages (3-5 bullets)
- Contact information

**Format**: 1 page, visually dense

**Example**: `SNOW_Investor_Fact_Sheet_FY2025.pdf`

## Tier 2: Supporting Materials

### 5. Customer Success Stories / Case Studies

**What**: 2-3 page PDFs highlighting customer wins

**Structure**:
- Customer logo and industry
- Challenge/problem statement
- Solution (how they use the platform)
- Results (metrics, ROI)
- Customer quote
- Technical details

**Examples**:
- "How Uber Saved $67M with Querybase" (QRYQ case study)
- "Netflix Scales ML with Voltaic AI" (VLTA case study)
- "Goldman Sachs Governance with CatalogX" (CTLG case study)

### 9. Investor Day Call Recordings (Non-Earnings)

**What**: Different from earnings calls - focused on strategy

**Types**:
- **Annual Investor Day** - Deep dive into strategy (once per year)
- **Technology Day** - Product and tech roadmap presentations
- **Analyst Day** - For sell-side analysts
- **M&A Announcement Calls** - If acquisitions happen

**Content**:
- CEO presents 5-year vision
- Product demos
- Customer testimonials (video/audio)
- Technology deep dives
- Q&A with multiple executives

**Example**: `SNOW_Investor_Day_2024.mp3` or `.m4a`

**Transcript**: `SNOW_Investor_Day_2024_Transcript.pdf`

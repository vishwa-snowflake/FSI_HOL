## Characteristics of "Low Performers"

These reports reflect realistic characteristics of smaller, high-growth SaaS companies:

### Common Traits

1. **High Growth but Unprofitable**
   - 156-287% revenue growth
   - -10% to -40% operating margins
   - Investing heavily in R&D and S&M

2. **Scaling Challenges**
   - Smaller customer bases (450-890)
   - Limited enterprise penetration
   - Lower average deal sizes

3. **Weak Unit Economics**
   - Negative free cash flow
   - High customer acquisition costs
   - Lower NRR than market leaders

4. **Competitive Pressure**
   - Facing larger, better-funded competitors
   - Limited differentiation
   - Market share challenges

5. **Limited IR Infrastructure**
   - No infographics or detailed reports
   - Minimal analyst coverage
   - Sparse corporate communications

### Why Include Them?

1. **Realism**: Not all companies are winners
2. **Diversity**: Show range of company maturity levels
3. **Comparison**: Highlight leaders vs challengers
4. **Analysis**: Enable "why do some companies outperform?" queries
5. **Decision-making**: Demonstrate investment screening criteria

# Company Stories Summary

This document summarizes the narrative arcs for the 8 core companies in the FSI Cortex Assistant demo.

## Core Company Narratives

### 1. **SNOW (Snowflake Inc.)** - The Resilient Leader
- **Role**: Market leader defending against threats
- **Key Events**:
  - Sept 19, 2024: Downgraded to SELL due to NRNT threat
  - Stock decline: -3% during crisis
  - CEO dismisses NRNT as unrealistic
  - Nov 20, 2024: Upgraded back to HOLD after NRNT collapse
  - Stock recovery: +6.6% by Nov 29
- **Revenue**: $900M, 29% YoY growth
- **Competitors**: ICBG (open lakehouse), QRYQ (price-performance)
- **Partners**: DFLX, STRM, VLTA, CTLG
- **Message**: Enterprise infrastructure beats consumer hype

### 2. **NRNT (Neuro-Nectar)** - The Spectacular Failure
- **Role**: Disruptive threat turned cautionary tale
- **Timeline**:
  - Aug 30, 2024: Peak ($142M revenue, 487% growth)
  - Sept 2024: Apex calls it "black swan" threat
  - Stock peak: $133.75
  - Sept-Nov: Gradual decline as issues emerge
  - Nov 15-20, 2024: **CRASH** (-90.4%)
  - Nov 20: **DELISTED** at $12.79
- **Cause**: Severe gastric distress from product
- **Duration**: 62 days from threat to extinction
- **Message**: Speculative hype meets harsh reality

### 3. **ICBG (ICBG Data Systems)** - The Open Alternative
- **Role**: Open-source challenger to SNOW
- **Position**: Apache Iceberg-based open lakehouse
- **Revenue**: $87M, 156% growth
- **Differentiation**:
  - vs SNOW: Openness, no vendor lock-in
  - vs QRYQ: DIY vs managed service
- **CEO Quote**: "Open architectures are more future-proof"
- **Partners**: DFLX, STRM, VLTA, CTLG
- **Message**: Customers own their data

### 4. **QRYQ (Querybase Technologies)** - The Aggressive Challenger
- **Role**: Price-performance disruptor
- **Funding**: $400M raised at $3B valuation
- **Revenue**: $94M, 287% growth
- **Claims**: 2x better price-performance than SNOW
- **Win Rate**: 37% vs SNOW in competitive deals
- **Position**: Managed service with open formats (best of both worlds)
- **CEO Quote**: "Enhanced analysts will want to process MORE data"
- **Message**: Openness without operational complexity

### 5. **DFLX (DataFlex Analytics)** - The Switzerland Platform
- **Role**: Neutral BI layer across all data platforms
- **Revenue**: $67M, 143% growth, first positive cash flow
- **Customers**: 1,847
- **Position**: "Single pane of glass" across SNOW, QRYQ, ICBG
- **Partnerships**: STRM (real-time), VLTA (ML insights), CTLG (governance)
- **CEO Quote**: "We're not in data storage - we extract insights"
- **Message**: Best-of-breed BI, platform-agnostic

### 6. **STRM (StreamPipe Systems)** - The Data Circulatory System
- **Role**: Real-time data movement infrastructure
- **Revenue**: $51M, 198% growth
- **Customers**: 723
- **Capabilities**: 300+ source connectors, multi-destination
- **Position**: Source and destination agnostic
- **Partners**: DFLX (dashboards), VLTA (streaming ML), CTLG (governance)
- **CEO Quote**: "Circulatory system of the modern data stack"

### 7. **VLTA (Voltaic AI)** - The AI-First Platform
- **Role**: Production AI infrastructure specialist
- **Revenue**: $73M, 312% growth (highest growth rate)
- **Customers**: 287 enterprise
- **ACV**: $512K (highest of all companies)
- **Capabilities**: Feature stores, MLOps, model serving

### 8. **CTLG (CatalogX)** - The Governance Layer

- **Compliance**: GDPR, CCPA, AI regulation

- **CEO Quote**: "Governance is no longer optional"
- **Economics**: 30-40% less than bundled governance
- **Message**: Making composable stacks enterprise-ready

## Interconnected Narrative

The 8 companies form an ecosystem:

**Data Platforms (compete)**:
- SNOW: Integrated proprietary
- ICBG: Open DIY lakehouse
- QRYQ: Managed with open formats

**Complementary Layers (partner with all)**:
- DFLX: BI/Visualization
- STRM: Real-time integration
- VLTA: AI/ML infrastructure
- CTLG: Governance

**Wildcard**:
- NRNT: Speculative threat (now defunct)

## Key Dates

- **Aug 22-30, 2024**: Earnings calls (all companies at or near peak)
- **Sept 19, 2024**: Apex downgrades SNOW, NRNT threat recognized
- **Sept-Nov 2024**: NRNT gradual decline, SNOW under pressure
- **Nov 15-20, 2024**: NRNT administration and collapse
- **Nov 20-29, 2024**: SNOW recovery, threat eliminated

## Usage for Dataset Creation

When creating new datasets, use these stories to:
1. **Maintain consistency** across earnings calls, analyst reports, and price data
2. **Generate realistic sentiment** based on competitive positioning
3. **Create cross-references** between companies naturally
4. **Reflect key events** (NRNT collapse, SNOW recovery)
5. **Show partnerships** (DFLX/STRM/VLTA/CTLG work with all platforms)
6. **Demonstrate competition** (SNOW vs ICBG vs QRYQ)

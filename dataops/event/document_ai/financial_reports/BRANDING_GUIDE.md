## Common Report Elements

### Page Layout:
- **Size**: Letter (8.5" x 11") = 816px x 1056px at 96 DPI
- **Margins**: 48px all sides
- **Content Width**: 720px
- **Gutter**: 24px between columns

### Standard Sections:
1. Cover Page - Full color with logo
2. Executive Summary - Key highlights
3. Financial Performance - Tables and charts
4. Business Highlights - Narrative content
5. Financial Statements - Simplified P&L, Balance Sheet
6. Outlook - Guidance and strategy

### Chart Specifications:
- **Width**: 600px maximum
- **Height**: 300-400px
- **Grid Lines**: Light gray (#E0E0E0)
- **Axes**: Company secondary color
- **Data**: Company primary color with gradients

### Typography Scale:
- **H1 (Cover)**: 48px, Bold
- **H2 (Section)**: 32px, Bold
- **H3 (Subsection)**: 24px, Bold
- **Body**: 14px, Regular
- **Caption**: 11px, Regular
- **Numbers (Large)**: 36px, SemiBold

---

**This branding guide ensures each company has a distinct, professional, and consistent visual identity across all financial reports.**

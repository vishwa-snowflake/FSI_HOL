## Table Structure for Document AI Extraction

Each report includes comprehensive financial tables ideal for table extraction:

### Standard Tables Across All Reports:
1. **Consolidated Statement of Operations** (P&L)
   - 5 columns: Q2 FY25, Q2 FY24, Q1 FY25, 6M FY25, 6M FY24
   - Revenue, cost of revenue, gross profit, operating expenses, operating income/loss
   - Net income/loss, EPS (basic & diluted), weighted avg shares

2. **Operating Expenses Breakdown**
   - R&D, Sales & Marketing, G&A by sub-category
   - Dollar amounts and % of revenue

3. **Consolidated Balance Sheets**
   - 3 columns: Current quarter, prior quarter, year-ago quarter
   - Assets, liabilities, stockholders' equity

4. **Consolidated Cash Flow Statement**
   - 4 columns: Q2 FY25, Q2 FY24, 6M FY25, 6M FY24
   - Operating, investing, financing activities

5. **Key Operating Metrics**
   - 5 quarters of trend data
   - Customers, ARR tiers, NRR, GRR, ACV

### EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

CatalogX delivered strong Q2 results with 89% year-over-year revenue growth, demonstrating continued enterprise demand for data governance, cataloging, and compliance solutions. As data complexity increases across multi-cloud environments, CatalogX provides the control plane that enterprises need to discover, govern, and secure their data assets.

**Key Financial Highlights:**
- Total Revenue: $142 million (up 89% YoY)
- Subscription Revenue: $135 million (95% of total)
- Gross Margin: 77%
- Operating Income: $18M (13% margin) - profitably growing
- Total Customers: 2,485 (up from 1,585, +57% YoY)
- Net Revenue Retention: 125%

**Business Highlights:**
- 900 net new customers in Q2
- Data assets cataloged: 12.5B tables/files (up 142% YoY)
- Strong partnerships with SNOW, ICBG, QRYQ, DFLX, STRM, VLTA
- Compliance automation revenue grew 185% YoY
- Average data sources per customer: 18.5 (up from 12.2)

**Market Position:**
Leading independent data governance platform serving as control plane for multi-platform data estates.

### STRATEGIC PRIORITIES

1. **Expand Compliance Automation**
   - Target: 50% of revenue from compliance by FY2026 (currently 42%)
   - Add 10 new compliance frameworks in H2 FY2025
   - Automated policy generation and enforcement

2. **Deepen Platform Partnerships**
   - Joint GTM with SNOW, ICBG, QRYQ
   - Governance layer for STRM (data pipelines)
   - Policy enforcement for VLTA (ML models)
   - Semantic layer governance with DFLX

3. **AI/ML Governance**
   - Model governance and explainability
   - Feature store cataloging and lineage
   - AI compliance (EU AI Act, etc.)
   - Partner with VLTA on MLOps governance

4. **Scale Enterprise Motion**
   - Target $1M+ deals (currently 5% of customers)
   - Industry-specific compliance packages (FSI, healthcare, retail)
   - Build enterprise success team (20 CSMs)

5. **Product Innovation**
   - Real-time policy enforcement
   - Data privacy automation (masking, tokenization)
   - Automated data classification (PII, PHI, PCI)
   - GraphQL for metadata API

6. **Margin Expansion**
   - Operating margin target: 21% by FY2027
   - Subscription gross margin to 80%
   - R&D leverage: 30% → 25% of revenue

Company Mission & Market Position

GameMetrics provides gaming analytics infrastructure for game developers and publishers. Our platform uses Querybase for player behavior data, StreamPipe for real-time game telemetry, and DataFlex for game studio dashboards. We serve 890+ customers including major game studios and mobile gaming companies, focusing on free-to-play and live service games where real-time analytics drive revenue optimization. With exceptional 287% year-over-year growth, we're the clear leader in gaming vertical analytics. However, we continue operating at a loss (-10% operating margin) as we invest heavily in product development and customer acquisition. Our 125% NRR demonstrates strong customer expansion, and we're approaching profitability with improving unit economics. The gaming industry's massive shift toward data-driven development and live operations creates significant tailwinds for our platform. Our modular architecture integrating best-in-class tools resonates with technically sophisticated game development teams.

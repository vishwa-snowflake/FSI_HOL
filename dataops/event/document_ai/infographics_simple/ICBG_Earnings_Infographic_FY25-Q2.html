Company Mission & Business Strategy

ICBG Data Systems delivers an open data lakehouse platform built on Apache Iceberg, championing openness and interoperability to eliminate vendor lock-in. Our mission is to democratize data infrastructure through open table formats that give customers full ownership and control of their data. We position ourselves as the 'Switzerland' of data platforms, differentiating from SNOW's proprietary ecosystem and QRYQ's managed service approach. Our strategic focus is on enterprise customers seeking future-proof architectures with the flexibility to avoid vendor dependency. We partner extensively with DFLX for BI, STRM for data ingestion, VLTA for ML workloads, and CTLG for governance, creating a best-of-breed ecosystem that prioritizes customer choice and architectural flexibility.

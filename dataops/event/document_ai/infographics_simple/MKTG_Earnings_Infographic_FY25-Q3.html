Company Mission & Competitive Challenges

Marketing Analytics provides marketing performance analytics and campaign attribution for small to mid-market businesses. While showing solid 156% revenue growth with 620+ customers, we face significant challenges including deteriorating operating margins (-40%, worst in sector), accelerating cash burn ($16M quarterly), and weak net revenue retention (105%, indicating limited customer expansion). We compete in a highly fragmented market against both large marketing clouds (Adobe, Salesforce) and numerous well-funded specialized vendors. Our lack of a broader marketing platform limits cross-sell opportunities and creates customer acquisition headwinds. Average revenue per customer remains modest, limiting near-term revenue potential. The company provides minimal investor materials beyond earnings calls, creating transparency concerns for institutional investors. Despite positive revenue growth, we face questions about competitive differentiation, path to profitability, and ability to compete against larger platforms with more comprehensive feature sets.

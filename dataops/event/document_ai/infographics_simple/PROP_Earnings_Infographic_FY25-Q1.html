Company Mission & Market Position

PropTech Analytics provides real estate data analytics for property managers, REITs, and real estate investors. Our platform leverages Querybase for property and market data, DataFlex for portfolio visualizations, and CatalogX for managing sensitive real estate information and compliance. We focus on commercial and residential property analytics including portfolio performance tracking, market trend analysis, occupancy optimization, and investment underwriting. While showing strong revenue growth (234% YoY), we continue to invest heavily in customer acquisition and product development, resulting in significant operating losses. Our 450+ customer base includes major REITs and property management firms, though we face intense competition from larger integrated platforms and struggle with enterprise penetration (only 18 customers above $1M ARR). Net revenue retention of 110% is positive but below industry leaders, indicating moderate expansion within our customer base.

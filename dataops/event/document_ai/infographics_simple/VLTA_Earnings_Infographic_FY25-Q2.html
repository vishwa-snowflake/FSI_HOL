COMPANY MISSION & BUSINESS STRATEGY

Voltaic AI is an AI-first data platform purpose-built for machine learning and AI workloads at production scale. Our mission is to provide the infrastructure layer that enables enterprises to build, train, deploy, and manage AI models at Netflix-scale and Uber-scale complexity. We offer comprehensive MLOps capabilities including feature stores, model training, model serving, and complete ML lifecycle management. Our strategic positioning both complements and competes with data warehouse ML capabilities‚Äîsimple ML in the warehouse works fine, but production AI at scale requires Voltaic's specialized infrastructure.

Investment Thesis

We maintain our BUY rating on Snowflake Inc. (SNOW) with increased conviction. Six months into the AI-driven growth cycle, execution has been flawless. The Intelligence Layer launch has not only met but exceeded our most optimistic projections. Customer adoption has been rapid, with over 2,500 enterprises now actively using AI features on the platform. The company's strategic positioning as the "AI Data Cloud" continues to resonate strongly in the enterprise market, leading to accelerated consumption and improved unit economics.

Recent Developments

Snowflake reported outstanding Q2 FY26 results on August 28, significantly surpassing all consensus estimates. Product revenue grew 38% YoY, well above the guided 30-32% range. The company raised FY26 guidance to 35% product revenue growth. Most notably, the Intelligence Layer has achieved remarkable traction, with AI-related consumption growing 150% sequentially. The platform now hosts over 400 enterprise AI applications, validating the company's strategic vision.

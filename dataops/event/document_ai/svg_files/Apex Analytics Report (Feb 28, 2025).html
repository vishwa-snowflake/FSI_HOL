Snowflake Inc. (SNOW)

Report Date: February 28, 2025

Analyst's Summary

Analysis by Eleanor Vance, CFA

INVESTMENT RATING: BUY

  • Strong Q4 results and encouraging FY26 guidance suggest a growth re-acceleration is underway.
  • The newly unveiled 'Snowflake Intelligence Layer' provides a credible and compelling strategy for capitalizing on the enterprise AI wave.
  • Product revenue growth of 32% in Q4 beat expectations, and the company posted better-than-expected operating margins.
  • We are raising our FY26 non-GAAP EPS estimate to $1.10 (from $0.88) and introducing our FY27 forecast of $1.55.

Investment Thesis

We are upgrading our rating on Snowflake Inc. (SNOW) to BUY from HOLD. The narrative has materially shifted over the past quarter. Concerns about decelerating growth are being replaced by optimism around a new, AI-driven product cycle. The company's strong Q4 performance and, more importantly, its clear vision for the 'Snowflake Intelligence Layer,' give us confidence that SNOW is poised to be a primary beneficiary of enterprise AI spending. While valuation remains a consideration, we believe the company's strategic position and renewed growth trajectory justify a premium multiple.

Recent Developments

Snowflake reported its fiscal Q4 2025 results on February 26, surpassing consensus estimates. Product revenue grew 32% YoY. For FY26, the company guided to 30% product revenue growth. At its "AI Day" in January, Snowflake provided the first detailed look at its 'Intelligence Layer,' a suite of tools that includes a managed LLM service and vector search. This has catalyzed a positive shift in investor sentiment.

Snowflake Inc. (SNOW)

Q2 FY26: AI Strategy Delivers Exceptional Growth; Raising Target to $325

Six months into the AI transformation, execution has been flawless. The Intelligence Layer has exceeded all expectations with 18% of consumption now AI-driven. Q2's exceptional 38% product revenue growth and raised FY26 guidance validate our thesis that Snowflake has evolved into the essential AI infrastructure platform. We see clear runway for sustained outperformance.

Key Takeaways

  • Maintaining BUY rating with increased conviction. Raising price target to $325 (from $255).
  • Q2 product revenue growth of 38% YoY significantly exceeded guidance range of 30-32%, with raised FY26 guidance to 35%.
  • The 'Intelligence Layer' has achieved remarkable adoption with over 2,500 enterprise customers and 400+ AI applications deployed.
  • Trading at 13x EV/NTM revenue, the valuation is reasonable given the platform's transformation into essential AI infrastructure.

Bottom Line

The AI transformation story continues to exceed expectations. Snowflake has successfully positioned itself as the foundational platform for enterprise AI, with the Intelligence Layer driving both consumption growth and competitive differentiation. The company's execution on its AI roadmap has been exceptional, with customer adoption surpassing even our most optimistic projections. With a clear path to sustained 35%+ growth and expanding margins, we believe SNOW remains one of the best positioned software names for the AI era.

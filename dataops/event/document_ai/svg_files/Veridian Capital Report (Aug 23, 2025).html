Executive Summary

We maintain our BUY rating on Snowflake and raise our price target to $330. Six months into the AI transformation, results have been nothing short of spectacular. Q2's 38% product revenue growth represents the highest growth rate in over two years, driven by explosive adoption of the Intelligence Layer. With AI workloads now contributing 18% of total consumption and over 2,500 enterprises actively deployed, the platform transformation is complete. The premium valuation is now fully justified by a clear path to sustained 35%+ growth, expanding margins, and an increasingly defensible competitive position.

The AI Transformation is Complete

The Intelligence Layer has exceeded every metric we established in February. Customer adoption has been remarkable, with enterprise deployments growing 300% quarter-over-quarter. More importantly, AI workloads are generating significantly higher consumption per customer, with average contract values increasing 45% year-over-year. The platform's ability to run AI workloads on governed, secure data has created a powerful competitive moat that traditional cloud providers cannot easily replicate. This positions Snowflake not just as a participant in the AI revolution, but as essential infrastructure that enables it.
